# Better Boilers

Semi-free-form multiblock steam boilers and turbines for Minecraft 1.12.2 (Forge).

Originally created by Boundarybreaker for elytra, relicensed to MIT and
released for adoption in 2020. Currently maintained and actively developed
by DrUltraLux.

## What it does

- **Boiler** - a scalable multiblock that burns fuel to convert water into
  steam. Build it as small or as large as you like; no fixed shape required
  beyond a few simple placement rules.
- **Turbine** - a redesigned multiblock generator: a shaft runs from the
  Controller to a Power Tap, with one or more rotor stages (2 or 4 blades
  each) along its length. Coils wrapped around each stage's blades convert
  captured steam into FE, with output scaling based on how many blades and
  how large a coil tier you use (Iron or Gold, with Copper support if
  Immersive Engineering is installed).

## Status

This is an actively maintained revival of the original project - the
toolchain, block roster, and turbine mechanics have all been substantially
rebuilt. See the changelog for details on what's changed.
